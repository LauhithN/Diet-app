Screen purposes:

HomeView
Shows daily overview including calorie progress and quick actions.

DailyMealPlanView
Displays meals for the day with calorie totals.

MealCardView
Displays a styled card with meal information.

GroceryListView
Displays ingredients needed for meal plans.

ExercisePlanView
Displays light exercise routines.

AIMealSuggestionView
Uses AI to generate meal suggestions.

ProgressView
Displays weight and calorie progress charts.

SettingsView
Allows configuration and preferences.

The UI must feel calm and supportive rather than technical.
